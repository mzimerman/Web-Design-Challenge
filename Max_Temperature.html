Max Temperature

Temperatures were the highest in cities located around the equator (0 Latitude). As expected, the lowest temperatures were observed in cities further from the equator in both hemispheres. However, warmest temperatures are found in the southern hemisphere. This is due to the data being collected in January coinciding with the tilt of the earth making the southern hemisphere closer to the sun.
